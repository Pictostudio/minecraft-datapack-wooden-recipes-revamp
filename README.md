## Brief
A datapack that rebalances wooden recipes within a vanilla friendly way, no more in getting just the one pressure plate from two planks. Instead receieve 4, as you can get 2 slabs from 1 plank this seems fair.

<img alt="recipe examples" src="https://wsrv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdb9og4cir%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fv1731618194%2FMinecraft%2Fdatapacks%2Fwrr%2Fnewrcipes.png&n=-1">

### Datapack Information
  - Rebalances vanilla wooden recipes, to give you more output in a balanced way.
  - Added a log to chest recipe, 8 logs to 4 chests!
  - Added a log to stick recipe, 2 logs to 16 sticks!
